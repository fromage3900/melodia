Melodia Simple PCG — scatter your meshes
=========================================

Path: /Game/_PROJECT/PCG/Graphs/Simple/

BUILD (editor console):
  Melodia.BuildSimplePCG

Or Python:
  py "G:/Melodia/Scripts/PCG/melodia_pcg_simple_builder.py"

USE (no custom kit required):
  1. Place a PCG Volume in your level
  2. PCG Component → Graph → pick a Simple graph below
  3. Resize volume to cover the area you want
  4. Generate (or enable Generate on Load)

GRAPHS
------
Volume voxel (works on flat ground or anywhere):
  PCG_Simple_WallScatter      — baroque wall modules (400cm spacing)
  PCG_Simple_ColumnScatter    — columns
  PCG_Simple_CorniceScatter   — cornice pieces
  PCG_Simple_TowerScatter     — tower accents (sparse)
  PCG_Simple_BridgeScatter    — bridge/corridor modules

Fixed grid (module blockout at origin of volume):
  PCG_Simple_WallGrid         — 400cm wall grid
  PCG_Simple_ColumnGrid       — 400cm column grid

Landscape (level needs Landscape actor):
  PCG_Simple_MeadowScatter    — ground cover from catalog
  PCG_Simple_RockScatter      — rocks on slope

Meshes come from Scripts/PCG/baroque_mesh_catalog.json collections.
Edit spawner node in graph or catalog JSON, then re-run BuildSimplePCG.
